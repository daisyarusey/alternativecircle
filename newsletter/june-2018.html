YOUR TAKE
IN THE
WORK PLACE

By Phidi Mwatibo.

It has been a whirlwind of events these past few weeks with the press hot on their heels discussing youth being blacklisted and over-indebtedness due to over borrowing. The finger is being pointed at micro-lenders for this "mess", and this puts me in a spot I love being in - crisis management and damage control PR. Instead of coming out on the defensive, I think it is time micro-lenders came together and found a way forward to this end. We need to educate our customers - especially the defaulters on responsible borrowing and consequences of late re-payments. What I have discovered is that most of these people have no idea what happens to them when they default until they jump on another lending platform and they are informed they have been black listed because they defaulted on another app. This gets them frustrated because they had no idea the CRB existed and even if they did, they didn't know that they would be black listed from borrowing and affect their credit score negatively. We are in the process of creating a blog with Kevin on Financial Literacy where we aim to share tips on how to manage finances, using micro-lending platforms to better a customer's life and consequences of having a negative credit score among other topics. We will be welcoming guest writers so feel free to email me any articles - p.mwatibo@alternativecircle.com around the topic, and who knows, you might end up on the opinion section of the newspaper as well! I truly believe that this first step will be the beginning of a better future for our users and future customers!
