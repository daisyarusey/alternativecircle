  • Our Mission

    To invest time, money and other resources to build $1 bn dollar businesses in high impact markets that create positive impact such as employment, wealth creation and hope to over 1 billion Africans by 2030.

Every story has a beginning...

Alternative Circle Technology Solutions has its roots in entrepreneurship.
The founding team has built several businesses over the last 16 years. The key insights gained were;

  1. Building big businesses requires the same effort needed to build an SME, so why not aim for the stars.
  2. Entrepreneurship can solve large social issues while creating lots of value for its investors.
  3. Every good game in existence is a long-term game. We look for opportunities that we can be a part of for over 10 years.

  • The 7th Generation Concept.

    The 7th Generation Concept dictates that every major decision a leader makes, the decision maker must always represent someone 7 generations from them and make decisions based on how it would impact that member 7 generations from them. Using this ethos, our current strategic plan envisions businesses in Digital Financial Services and Technology Solutions, New Age Media, Sports Entertainment, Real Estate and Sustainable Energy.
